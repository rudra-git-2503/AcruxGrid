Employee Sentiment Analysis

Case Study: Global Workforce Insights via NLP

Client Overview

A large BFSI Client partnered with AcruxGrid to mine employee surveys for deep sentiment and topic patterns using transformer-based NLP models.

Challenges

  • Manual survey analysis missed core concerns
  • Lack of sentiment insights across regions
  • Delayed understanding of employee morale trends
  • No thematic clustering of feedback

Our Approach

  • Applied BERT for multi-lingual sentiment analysis
  • Implemented topic modeling across feedback sets
  • Mapped emotional trends over geography & time
  • Delivered HR dashboards with actionables
